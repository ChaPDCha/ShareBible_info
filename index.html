RealBible Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

RealBible collects the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address and name (when signing in with Google)
  • App Usage Data: Bible passages read, bookmarks, and search history
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and advertising ID

2. Purpose of Data Collection

  • To manage and authenticate user accounts
  • To sync reading progress and bookmarks across devices
  • To analyze and improve app performance
  • To provide personalized advertisements

3. Third-Party Services

RealBible uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Account authentication, data storage, and analytics
  • Google AdMob: Ad display and monetization

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Account Information: Retained until the account is deleted
  • App Usage Data: Retained for up to 2 years
  • Account Deletion Request: Contact us at musicbox260@gmail.com

5. User Rights

Users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data at any time.

6. Children’s Privacy

RealBible does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Security

All data is transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption.

9. Account & Data Deletion Request

RealBible (Developer: ChaPD) respects your privacy and gives you full control over your data.

How to Request Account Deletion

  • In-App Deletion: Open RealBible > Settings > User Settings > Tap 'Withdraw' or 'Delete Account'.
  • Email Request: If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing musicbox260@gmail.com with your registered email address.

Data Types Deleted and Retention Period

  • Deleted Data: Account email, nickname, profile info, bible bookmarks, notes, highlights, and all reading history associated with your account.
  • Retention Period: Upon requesting deletion, all your personal data is immediately and permanently deleted from our active databases. (Data residing in automated system backups may be retained for up to 30 days before being completely purged).
