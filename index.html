ShareBible Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2025

1. Information We Collect

ShareBible collects the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address and name (when signing in with Google)
  • App Usage Data: Bible passages read, bookmarks, and search history
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and advertising ID

2. Purpose of Data Collection

  • To manage and authenticate user accounts
  • To sync reading progress and bookmarks across devices
  • To analyze and improve app performance
  • To provide personalized advertisements

3. Third-Party Services

ShareBible uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Account authentication, data storage, and analytics
  • Google AdMob: Ad display and monetization

4. Data Retention and Deletion
